# Decathlon Racket Sports Analytics & Inventory Prediction System

## Objective

To analyze sales performance, inventory health, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and store performance across Decathlon's Racket Sports department.

## Business Problem

Store managers need visibility into:

* High-performing products
* Slow-moving products
* Inventory shortages
* Overstock situations
* Store-wise performance
* Future product demand
